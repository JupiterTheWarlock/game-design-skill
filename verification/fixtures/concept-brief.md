# Concept validation fixture

- Developer: solo developer with six months for a playable PC demo.
- Desired experience: tense exploration that repeatedly turns fear into mastery.
- Core fantasy: maintain a fragile walking lighthouse while crossing a drowned world.
- Preferred play: short expeditions, route planning, environmental problem-solving, and returning with discoveries.
- Avoid: combat-heavy progression, endless procedural content, daily retention obligations, and competitive multiplayer.
- Art constraint: stylized low-poly 3D with a small content budget.
- Validation need: define a focused concept, three useful pillars with design tests, anti-pillars, and nested gameplay loops without inventing market facts.
